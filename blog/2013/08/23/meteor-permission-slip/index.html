Permission Slip

How the app works:

Admin 1. Create a slip 2. Email parents link to slip

Parents 1. Parent gets email 2. Clicks link 3. Fills in details 4. Clicks yes or no

Took the parties example and modified it, plus added routing, tokens and “guest account.”
